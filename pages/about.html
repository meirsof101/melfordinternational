Our Story

Melford International Express Gateway, trading as Melford International Group is a trusted educational and migration service provider based in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya. Established as a government-registered company, we are dedicated to offering top-quality educational guidance to students seeking opportunities abroad.

Melford International is deeply committed to integrity, reliability, and unwavering support for students pursuing international education. Since our establishment in 2021, Melford International has been privileged to support students in achieving their academic goals worldwide. With more than three (3) years of experience, we have built partnerships with respected universities and colleges in the United Kingdom, Canada, New Zealand, Australia, Europe, UAE, and beyond.

Our approach combines modern facilities with the guidance of experienced counsellors who provide personalized advice, making the journey easier and more transparent for our clients. Watching our students thrive and achieve their dreams is a constant source of pride and inspiration for us at Melford International Group.

A Message From Our Director

Welcome to Melford International Group.

I wholeheartedly align with the timeless wisdom of Nelson Mandela, who once said, "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." This truth holds even greater significance when education is pursued in a forward-thinking, globally connected environment—one that nurtures innovation, cultural exchange, and boundless opportunity.

At Melford International Group, we offer you a precise pathway to your educational dreams. Through our partnerships with top-tier universities and colleges across five premier study destinations—Australia, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Canada—you gain access to world-class education that prepares you for a dynamic global future.

We wish you every success as you embark on this remarkable journey, and we thank you for entrusting Melford International Group with your future.
