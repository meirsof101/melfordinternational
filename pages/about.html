Our Journey

Milestones in our educational mission

2005: Humble Beginnings

Founded in London by Dr. Evelyn Melford with a vision to bridge educational gaps between countries. Our first office opened with just 3 staff members and a passion for global education.

2010: Expansion to Asia

Established our first regional office in Singapore, marking our expansion into the Asian market. Partnered with 50+ universities across the UK, USA, and Australia.

2014: Scholarship Program Launch

Created the Melford Global Scholars initiative, providing over $2M in scholarships to deserving students from developing countries. Awarded "Education Innovator of the Year" by Global Education Network.

2018: Digital Transformation

Launched our comprehensive online portal, enabling students to access services remotely. Developed AI-powered matching system for university placements.

2023: Global Recognition

Celebrated placing our 15,000th student. Recognized as "Top Education Consultancy" by International Education Awards for three consecutive years.
